Our Story

Decode the Interview (DTI) was founded to help students navigate through the technical recruiting process for software engineering positions. Quick insights into what we offer:

  • Mock interview sessions with our experienced student leads who have nailed the interview process and received offers from Google, Amazon, Microsoft, etc.
  • Workshops from behavioral interview preparation to tackling popular coding problems.
  • Networking and other opportunities through our collaboration with the Notre Dame Career Center.

Mock Interviews

There is no better way to prepare for coding interviews than having a mock interview.

Our experienced student leads offer one-on-one mock interview sessions, which you can register through Handshake.

Here are 3 simple steps to register a mock interview:

1. Go to the Handshake Appointment link

2. Click on "Industry Specific One-on-One Career Counseling (Virtual Appointments)"

3. Scroll to the bottom and click on Decode the Interview Technical Mock Interviews
